My Trip to Port Alsworth, Alaska & Anchorage Alaska

My trip to Ancorage Alaska was a trip of a lifetime. I will show key highlights of the trip. I went on the trip with two friend Tim and Evan. We had to fly to Anchorage and then take a plane out to main destination of Port Alsworth. Port Alsworth is west of Ancorage and has 100 year round residents. It was the first time in Alaska for all three of us.

Leaving Anchorage

The first adventure of the trip was the flight to Port Alsworth. The plane was a tiny twin engine aircraft. We had to have the weight of our bags be exact. When packing for the trip we had to plan for this flight.

Arrival in Port Alsworth

There were many float planes. There was a tiny gravel landing strip as well.

Hike to remote lake

We had an eleven mile hike to this lake. It was a tough hike as there was no trails which made you have to watch each step carefully. It was beautiful as were able to pass through multiple biomes. I especially enjoyed walking through the frozen tundra.

Return to civilization

After the trip we had fours days in Anchorage. We did not have any plans besides staying at this youth hostel. The only room we could reserve was one with multiple roomates. We ended up biking around town, fishing and exploring the town.
